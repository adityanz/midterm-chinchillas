Midterm Chinchillas

By: Ned Azar, Enrique Bascur, and Jaden Hamid

In this midterm project we will be visualizing the SFFD (San Francisco Fire Dept.) calls for service data set from three different angles.

Our Theme

For this project, we decided to look at the change in the SFFD dataset over 5 years. We hope to find any outliers or trends from 2015 to 2019. We are looking at different aspects of response times from the time Date and time the 911 operator dispatches this unit to the call, to the time when the unit arrives on scene.
